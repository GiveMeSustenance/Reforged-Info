Perk

After dealing 300 dmg in a row to a single target, spawns 5 shadows dealing 30 dmg each to attack it. Summons, companions, and fire staff special do not count towards the 300 dmg but can reset it if they damage a different enemy.

Shadows

Shadow damage can be influenced by

  • Debuffs on enemies

  • Buffs on Maxwell including Cultist Staff - Grow Allies, Attack Spice, Wigfrid Battle Cry (if gained after the attack that triggers them), and Hex Darts (idk only if used by max).

  • Armor including Corrupted Wreath, Assassin's Hood, Cultist Cloak
